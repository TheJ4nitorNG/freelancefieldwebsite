Information & FAQ

Everything you need to know about working with experts on Freelance Field.

How do I hire an expert?
Simply browse our categories or the main directory, find the expert whose skills match your project needs, and click "Hire". You'll be prompted to message them directly to start scoping your project.
Are the freelancers vetted?
Yes. Every professional on Freelance Field, from corporate lawyers to mobile developers, undergoes a strict verification process to ensure their credentials and previous work meet our high standards.
How do payments work?
Payments are held safely in escrow until the project milestones are completed to your satisfaction. You can pay via credit card, ACH, or wire transfer.
Can I apply to be a freelancer?
Absolutely. Click the "Join as Freelancer" button at the top right to create your account. Once your profile is reviewed and approved, you can start accepting clients.
